**Two Ways to Use Church Center: Website and App**

1. Access via website: <https://kaleoalaska.churchcenter.com/home>
2. Download the App: Available free for Apple and Android. See Links below.

 We love the Church Center App and all that you can do through it:

* Check-in your children in on Sunday mornings
* Manage your giving
* Register for events
* Access your K-Group or browse and sign up for a K-Group
* Access our members directory

It puts the things you need right in the palm of your hand. [Watch Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vr9fFXkfWsg)

**Download & Setup the App:**

Simply download the Church Center App from your App Store (available free for Apple and Android) and follow the instructions to allow Church Center to use your location to find the Kaleo Church. Choose Kaleo Church, click “This is my church.” Finish the prompts to log in for the first time and select your profile. If you don’t already have a profile in our system, follow the prompts to create one.

## K-Groups

*The "K" in K-Groups stands for [koinonia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Koinonia) which means "communion" or "fellowship" in greek. K-Groups are one of the primary ways people of Kaleo connect with one another.*

#### To See what K-Groups are available, click the button below.

We encourage you to explore a few different k-groups before you commit to one so that you can connect with our community.

[![KGROUP](https://cpmfiles1.com/kaleoalaska.org/kgroup.png)](https://kaleoalaska.churchcenter.com/groups/k-groups?enrollment=open_signup%2Crequest_to_join&filter=enrollment)

#### Interested in leading a k-group?

We are always on the lookout for more K-Group Leaders. If you are interested in exploring that idea, we would love to chat. For more details or to start that discussion, check out our **[K-Group Leader Job Description.](https://cpmfiles1.com/kaleoalaska.org/k-group-leader-job-description-r3.pdf)**

### ***All your children shall be taught by the Lord,******and great shall be the peace of your children.*** ***~Isaiah 54:13***

Children are precious to God and to us here at Kaleo. What children choose to believe about God is the most important thing about them. Our goal is to partner with parents to teach children who God is and what He has done. We pray that children would put their faith and hope in Jesus, so that they would have true joy and peace.

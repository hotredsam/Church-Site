## Children's Director

Lucy has lived in Alaska since elementary school. She graduated from San Diego Christian College in 2004 with a degree in Bible and Psychology. She is married to Patrick and they have 4 children.

She has a background in administrative work, youth ministry, basketball, and running. Her crazy dream is to be a farmer and she loves all things old-school country.

She is thankful for life in Jesus and her family. God's power in creation and redemption will never cease to amaze her.

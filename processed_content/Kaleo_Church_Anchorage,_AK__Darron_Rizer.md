# **Elder**

Darron was born and raised in Anchorage and graduated from Service High School.  He met his wife, Jennifer, while earning a B.A. in Business Management at Abilene Christian University in Texas. Post-grad, he completed a Master’s of Science in Financial Services (MSFS). Jenny and Darron were married in Arlington, Texas, December 2000.

Six months after marriage, Darron brought Jenny back to Anchorage with him where they planted roots and began a family. God blessed them with four children. He has also been blessed in his career as a financial advisor since graduating college.

Darron looks forward to spending quality time with his family whenever possible. He is active at church with his continued involvement Kaleo Men’s Ministry. During his free time, He loves spending time in the great outdoors hunting, fishing, hiking, boating and camping. Darron was among the Kaleo church plant launch team from Cornerstone Church and has served in K-group and men’s ministry from the beginning.

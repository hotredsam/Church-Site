## Cody Portrait

## Associate Pastor & Elder

Cody was born and raised in Anchorage and graduated from Service High School. He met his wife while earning a B.S. in Math, Science and Aviation at Central Christian College of Kansas. They were married in 2001.

A year after marriage, Cody brought Sara back to Anchorage with him where they began fostering children and eventually adopting from foster care. Through a combination of fostering, adopting and natural born, they have 9 children. He has worked in various industries including construction and engineering.

If not at the church office, you can expect Cody to be found renovating some part of his house, wood-working in his garage, or hunting and fishing. Cody was among the Kaleo church plant launch team from Cornerstone Church and has served first as an elder and Associate Pastor since 2016.

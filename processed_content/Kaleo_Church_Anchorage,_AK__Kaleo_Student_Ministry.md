## KNOWING JESUS AND MAKING HIM KNOWN

Kaleo Student Ministry (KSM) serves 6th-12th grade students. Our mission is all about knowing Jesus and making Him known. To accomplish this, KSM seeks to live life resolved:

* We are resolved to put prayer first in our ministry and individual lives.
* We are resolved to care well for others inside and outside of our ministry and church.
* We are resolved to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ no matter the cost.

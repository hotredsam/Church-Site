**Purpose:**

Men's ministry at Kaleo sets out to accomplish two goals:  
1) Call up men into Godly disciples of Jesus, and 2) bond men closer to each other in the church through serving and learning together.

*Iron sharpens iron,*  
*and one man sharpens another.*   
            - Proverbs 27:17

Kaleo men gather together on the first Monday of each month to eat, have fun, and intentionally study scripture and how it applies to their own lives.

**How to Join:**

1. To see the upcoming schedule and sign-up,   
[Follow this link to the sign-up page](https://kaleoalaska.churchcenter.com/groups/life-stage-and-demographic-groups/men-s-ministry)

2. For regular updates and to communicate with others in the group,   
[Download the Church Center App.](https://www.kaleoalaska.org/church-center-instructions)

3. Join us on the next "First Monday" of the month from 6-8PM at the church building.   
[1301 W. 100th Ave. Anchorage, AK 99515](https://maps.app.goo.gl/AwzVGX7vuXRnSupV9)

**Frontline Men:**

**![Front Line Men](https://cpmfiles1.com/kaleoalaska.org/front-line-men.png)**

Kaleo Frontline Men are ready to serve if you have found yourself in need of light home or auto maintenance. Some of our men have volunteered to invest an hour or two a month into helping our community. If you have a need, please take a minute to fill out THIS FORM. If you are part of the Kaleo Men's Ministry and would like to serve in this way, please [APPLY HERE](https://kaleoalaska.churchcenter.com/people/forms/766443) to join Frontline Men.

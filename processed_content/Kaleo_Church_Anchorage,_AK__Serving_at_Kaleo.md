Did you know God has already planned for you to make an impact?

> *“For we are His workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works, which God prepared beforehand, that we should walk in them."  (Ephesians 2:10)*

Maybe you’re ready to start simply – and simply start! Or maybe you’re looking for a better fit or more impactful opportunity. Either way, you’re in the right place.

Below you will find several opportunities to serve in the areas where we have current needs. If you are looking to serve in a way not listed, please reach out to us at the email address at the bottom of this page.

All postions are categorized by their ministry area. Click any of the available positions to open its job description and learn more.

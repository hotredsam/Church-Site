Advent comes from the Latin word meaning, "coming or arrival," and is a season marked on the church calendar beginning four Sundays before Christmas. It is a time in which believers around the world purposely turn their attention to their longing for Jesus. We remember His first Advent and hope for His second Advent.

To encourage your hearts this season, we have provided a daily devotional that can be used by individuals or families, as well as a weekly activity guide for families or groups to enjoy together. Click the links below to download a PDF of each.

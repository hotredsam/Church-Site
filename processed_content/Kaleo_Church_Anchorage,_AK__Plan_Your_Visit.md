## Visit Kaleo this Sunday at 11:45 am!

Whether you're exploring your faith for the first time, are new to the Christian faith, or have been a Jesus follower for years, we invite you to join us at Kaleo Church in Anchorage, Alaska. We offer a single service on Sundays at 11:45 am.

#### What to expect

Our service include family worship, where all ages are invited to join in our call to worship activities through prayer and song. Prior to the sermon, we dismiss our children from Nursery through 5th grade to enjoy [Kaleo Kids Ministry.](/kaleo-kids)

Our sermon messages are brought by a team of elders and teachers led by our [Pastor Chris Cha](/chris-cha).

Kaleo is a casual, authentic environment. You can wear jeans and a t-shirt or your Sunday best. Most of us dress for comfort. We want you to feel comfortable, too. Kaleo invites you to come as you are.

#### Location & MAP

Kaleo Church shares a building with Kingdom Life Church, and is located at [1301 W. 100th Ave](https://maps.app.goo.gl/hmkgUK39pQ8Juo3n8) in Anchorage.

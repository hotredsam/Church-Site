## **Lead Pastor and Elder**

Chris was born and raised in Honolulu, Hawaii.  He moved to Anchorage at the start of his junior year and graduated from East High School (go T-birds!).

Chris married the love of his life, Anna, in September of 1999 and now have six wonderful children. Chris graduated from Dallas Christian College with a degree in Ministry & Leadership, and is completing a master’s degree in Global Leadership at Western Seminary.

He loves spending time with his wife and kids, watching movies, eating good food, playing board games, playing and watching basketball, and reading.  His life verse is [John 3:30](https://biblia.com/bible/esv/John%203.30), “He must increase, but I must decrease.” Chris is most thankful for the abounding love of the Father, the amazing grace of Jesus Christ, the gracious fellowship of the Holy Spirit, Anna Cha, each of his children, and the Kaleo family.
